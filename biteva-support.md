## Quick Help

### I cannot register a product

Make sure the product name is not empty and that the expiry date has been selected correctly.

### The camera does not detect the expiry date correctly

To improve detection:

- hold the package straight in front of the camera
- avoid glare and reflections
- use good lighting
- try to keep the printed date inside the guide area

If the detected date is incorrect, you can correct it manually before saving.

### I am not receiving notifications

Please check:

- notifications are enabled in Biteva settings
- system notification permission has been granted
- battery saving or system restrictions are not blocking alerts

### Recipes are not appearing

Please verify:

- you have selected products
- you have available coins
- the recipe service connection is working correctly

### I cannot buy more coins

Please verify:

- you are signed in correctly to the device store
- in-app purchases are allowed
- internet access is available

If the payment was completed and your balance did not update, contact support and include the purchase details.

## Known Issues

Under certain lighting conditions or on packaging with poor print quality, automatic date detection may require manual correction.

On older physical devices or on system versions not fully aligned with the development environment, debug deployment may require additional Xcode setup. This does not necessarily affect normal store-distributed app usage.
