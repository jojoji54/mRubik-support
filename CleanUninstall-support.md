## Report an issue

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:
- App version (**CleanUninstall**)
- Mac model and macOS version
- Exact steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots (without sensitive data)
- Exact error message shown in the app
- Whether **Full Disk Access** is enabled for CleanUninstall

## Privacy & data

CleanUninstall scans files locally on your Mac and removes only the items you select.

- No account is required to use core app features.
- Purchase status is handled by Apple (Apple ID / StoreKit).
- For privacy/data questions, contact **mrrubikdev@gmail.com**.

---

## Purchases & restore

**CleanUninstall Pro** is a one-time unlock.

If your Pro unlock is not showing:
1. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase.
2. Open the app and tap **Restore Purchases**.
3. Reopen the app and verify Pro status.
4. If it still does not appear, email support with a screenshot of your purchase history.

---

## Full Disk Access

For deeper detection of leftover files, enable **Full Disk Access**:

- macOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Full Disk Access
- Enable access for **CleanUninstall**
- Return to the app and tap **Check**
