Report an issue

Account & privacy

Planazo uses your account to sync plans and groups across devices.
If you need to delete your account or have questions about your data, email us at mrrubikdev@gmail.com
 and include the email address you used to sign up.

Purchases & restore

If you purchased a subscription or pack and it doesn’t show up:

Make sure you’re using the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

In the paywall screen, tap “Restore Purchases”.

If it still doesn’t appear, contact us with your Apple receipt or a screenshot of your purchase history.
