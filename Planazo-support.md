## Report an issue

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:
- App version
- Device and operating system (iOS + version)
- Exact steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots (no sensitive data)
- If possible: logs or the exact error message shown in the app

## Account & privacy

Planazo uses your account to sync plans and groups across devices.

If you need to delete your account or have questions about your data, email **mrrubikdev@gmail.com** and include the email address you used to sign up.

---

## Purchases & restore

If you purchased a subscription or pack and it doesn’t show up:
1. Make sure you’re using the same Apple ID used for the purchase.
2. In the paywall screen, tap **Restore Purchases**.
3. If it still doesn’t appear, contact us and include your Apple receipt or a screenshot of your purchase history.
